Handwritten Digit Recognition

This project focuses on the recognition of handwritten digits
The primary objective is to develop a machine learning model capable of accurately predicting numerical digits (0 to 9) from input images.
To enhance usability and visual interaction, a web-based interface will be integrated,
allowing users to upload individual images and receive the corresponding predicted digit as output.
